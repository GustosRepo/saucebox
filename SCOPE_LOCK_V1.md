# Hot Packet V1 Scope Lock

## Status

Locked for V1 development.

No additional features are added unless they improve first-use sound quality, stability, or conversion.

## Free (V1) Included

1. Core texture engine.
2. Instant Sauce mode:
   - One large center macro knob.
   - Mix control.
   - Output control.
3. The Instant Sauce macro drives the existing chain behind the scenes: Drive, Texture, Wow Depth, Wow Rate, Tone, and Mix behavior.
4. Output safety chain:
   - Gain compensation behavior tied to drive/mix.
   - Soft output limiting.
5. Starter preset behavior and baseline preset bank (target: 12 presets).
6. Stable standalone and plugin startup on macOS.

## Pro (V1) Target Locks

1. Full detailed control unlock: Drive, Texture, Wow Depth, Wow Rate, Tone, Mix, Output.
2. Expanded preset bank and favorites.
3. Advanced shaping modules (transient/stereo/multiband texture).
4. Oversampling (2x/4x).
5. A/B compare.
6. Additional macros and deeper workflow tools.

## Not In Free V1

1. Direct access to individual Drive, Texture, Wow Depth, Wow Rate, and Tone controls.
2. Advanced shaping modules.
3. Oversampling.
4. A/B compare.
5. Extended preset management features.

## Conversion Path (V1)

1. Subtle in-plugin CTA for track review.
2. Link to a short lead form.
3. Follow-up process for service conversion.
