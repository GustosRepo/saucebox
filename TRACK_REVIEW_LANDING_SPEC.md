# Track Review Landing Page Spec (V1)

## Purpose

Convert Hot Packet users into qualified service leads with a low-friction free review offer.

## URL

Recommended production URL:

- https://saucebox.audio/review

## Above-the-fold Copy

### Headline

Get a Free Track Review

### Subheadline

I will send focused, actionable feedback to help your mix hit harder and translate better.

### Promise bullets

1. 3 to 5 practical notes you can apply immediately.
2. Honest feedback on tone, balance, and impact.
3. Optional next-step offer if you want me to finish it with you.

## Form Fields (Required)

1. Name
2. Email
3. Song Link (SoundCloud, Dropbox, Google Drive, etc.)
4. What are you stuck on? (short textarea)

## Form Fields (Optional)

1. Genre
2. Reference track link
3. Deadline

## CTA Button

- Primary label: Send My Track
- Secondary helper text: Usually replies within 24 to 48 hours.

## Submission Behavior

1. Show success state immediately.
2. Send auto-response email confirmation.
3. Add lead to follow-up pipeline (tag: saucebox-review).

## Auto-Response Email (Draft)

Subject: Got your track - review coming soon

Body:

Thanks for sending your track.

I will listen and send focused feedback (usually within 24 to 48 hours).

If I think I can help further, I will include clear options for next steps.

- 808Bytes Hot Packet

## Follow-Up Template (Manual)

1. Start with one positive observation.
2. Give 3 concrete fixes in priority order.
3. Offer one paid service path with timeline and price range.
4. End with a simple reply CTA: Want me to handle this for you?

## Design Notes

1. Keep page minimal, mobile-first, and fast.
2. No nav clutter.
3. Keep social proof short (1 to 2 testimonials max).
4. Preserve the Sauce Box visual identity.
